# Demo Script

Use this as a guide for a 3-5 minute video.

## 0:00 - 0:30 Problem

Recruiters lose hours reading profiles and chasing candidates who may not be interested. ScoutFlow AI turns one JD into an actionable ranked shortlist.

## 0:30 - 1:00 Input

Paste the sample JD for a Full Stack AI Engineer. Explain that the recruiter only needs to provide the job description.

## 1:00 - 1:40 JD Parsing

Show the parsed role, skills, minimum experience, work mode, and domain signals.

## 1:40 - 2:30 Ranked Shortlist

Click `Run Talent Scout`. Show the shortlist table with Match Score, Interest Score, and Final Score.

## 2:30 - 3:30 Explainability

Open the top candidate. Explain:

- Which skills matched
- Why the candidate is relevant
- Whether experience and availability fit
- Why the system recommends follow-up

## 3:30 - 4:20 Outreach Simulation

Show the generated recruiter message and simulated candidate reply. Explain how this creates an Interest Score.

## 4:20 - 5:00 Closing

Show CSV/JSON exports and architecture diagram. Mention production upgrades: GitHub discovery, ATS integration, vector DB, and real conversational outreach.
